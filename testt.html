eeyora is a post-rock, noise band led by frontman air

the band was formed by air

after the release of shirindarica (2023), recorded in his room

the 4 regular members - L, Oliver, Air and Margosha - regularly collaborate

with other musicians under the Am Stram Gram collective


eeyora concluded its first fully self-managed concert

on December 16th, 2023 in the center of Brussels,

along with fellow Am Stram Gram artists
